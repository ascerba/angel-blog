About

A short intro to Ángel

Aspiring actor, home cook, brand new father; none of these phrases describe Ángel Castañeda.

The genuine me is quite a bit more boring. I am an outgoing but nerdy twenty-year-old actuarial science (basically math) student from the great state of Wisconsin. I work at my family’s business as a tax preparer and notary.

Math is my main passion. It’s the most fun subject to figure out stuff by far. I am studying for exam P to move up in the actuary world. I am also learning real analysis on my own with a borrowed copy of Yet Another Introduction to Analysis by Victor Bryant.

Reading books is something I really enjoy. Classic western literature and more contemporary literature are my favorites. In 2021 I started writing reviews for some of the books I read.

History is my other great love, especially from the 18th century to the present day. I am in the process of starting a podcast with my former history teacher this fall about political history, so stay tuned!

I also like rock, jazz, amateur photography, milksteaks, talking to new people & making them laugh, building PCs, and learning Deutsch.

Here is my site to occasionally document what I do while coasting through the world. Email me at angelaxel1420@gmail.com if I made a horrible mistake or said something you thought was interesting or if you just want to chat.
