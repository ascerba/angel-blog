A short intro to Ángel

Aspiring actor, protégé chess player, brand new father; none of these phrases describe Ángel Castañeda.

The genuine me is a bit more boring. I am a twenty-year-old actuarial science (basically math) student from the state of Wisconsin. I work at my family's business as a tax preparer and notary.

Math is my main passion. I am studying for exam P to get my future fellowship. I am also learning real analysis on my own and trying to build proficiency in LaTeX. Overleaf is king.

Reading books is something I really enjoy. Classic literature and more recent fiction are my favorites. In 2021, I started writing reviews for some of the books I read that you can check out here.

Learning history is also something I do, especially from the 18th century to the present day. I am in the process of starting a podcast with my former history teacher about American political history, but please be patient. I am very good at delaying my projects with believable excuses. I am also really busy.

I also like jazz, rock, photography, milksteak, talking to passionate people, learning Deutsch, and burning my money on overpriced lenses and computer parts.

Here is my site to occasionally post about what I do while cruising down life. Email me at angelaxel1420@gmail.com in case you find an error or want me to clarify or discuss anything.

Prost! B-)
