A short intro to Ángel

Aspiring artist, home cook, brand new father; none of these phrases describe Ángel Castañeda.

The genuine me is quite a bit more boring. I am an outgoing but nerdy nineteen-year-old math student from the great state of Wisconsin. I work at my family’s business as a tax preparer and notary.

Math is my main passion. I am studying for exam P to move up in the actuary world.

Reading books is something I really enjoy. I started writing reviews for some of the books I read in 2021.

History is my other great love, especially from the 18th century to the present day.

I also like rock and traveling and making people laugh and talking to new people.

Here is my site to occasionally document what I do while coasting through the world. Email me at angelaxel1420@gmail.com if I made a horrible mistake or said something you thought was interesting or if you just want to chat.
